Frequently Asked Questions

LWS Festava 2025 takes place from September 15-17, 2025 at the iconic Red Rocks Amphitheatre in Colorado. This breathtaking natural venue provides the perfect backdrop for our premium music festival experience.

Early Bird ($199): 3-day general admission, access to all main stages, free festival merchandise, and food & drink discounts.

VIP Experience ($349): All Early Bird perks plus VIP viewing areas, fast-track entry, exclusive VIP lounge access, premium bars & gourmet food, artist meet & greet opportunities, and dedicated concierge service.

Our 2025 lineup features incredible artists including Luna Rivers (Pop/Alternative Rock), Neon Atlas (R&B/Pop/Electronic), and Zara Wilde (Alternative Pop/Electropop). We'll also have DJ Voltage bringing electronic beats and surprise special guests throughout the three-day experience.

Bring comfortable shoes, sunscreen, a refillable water bottle, and layers for changing weather. Don't forget your valid ID and festival wristband! Prohibited items include outside food/drinks, professional cameras, and glass containers. Check our full guidelines for a complete list.

Yes! Red Rocks offers several parking options. General parking is available on-site for $25 per day, with VIP parking closer to the venue for $50 per day. We also recommend carpooling or using ride-share services to reduce traffic and environmental impact.

All ticket sales are final, but we offer ticket transfers through our secure platform. If the event is cancelled due to circumstances beyond our control, full refunds will be issued. We recommend purchasing travel insurance for additional protection.
